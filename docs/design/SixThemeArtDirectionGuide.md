# Six Theme Art Direction Guide

- Hikmet: medrese geometry, emerald, muted gold, calm ivory/night green.
- Bozkır: steppe horizon, sun, stone, steel navy and copper.
- Eşitlik: solidarity rings, plum/fuchsia/coral, inclusive modern confidence.
- Tabiat: botanical branch and leaf linework, forest/sage/wood accents.
- Atlas: retro-futurist Art Deco skyline, sky city light mode and ocean metropolis dark mode.
- Avangard: broken grid, asymmetric blocks, controlled high-contrast graphic accents.

Eden logo remains fixed in every theme.
