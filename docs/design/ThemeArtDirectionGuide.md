# Theme Art Direction Guide

Date: 2026-06-08

## Classic

Neutral, safe and quiet. It is the fallback baseline and should not feel decorative.

## Art Deco Premium

Executive and architectural. Use ivory, graphite/navy, controlled gold accents and thin geometric linework.

## Anadolu 60'lar

Warm, local and modernist. Use cream, terracotta/copper, petrol and retro sun/ray motifs.

## Green Atelier

Natural, productive and crafted. Use paper, forest green, sage, muted amber and botanical linework.

## Pop Studio

Energetic and graphic, but still enterprise-safe. Use magenta, warm cream, controlled blue/orange accents and compact graphic corner details.
